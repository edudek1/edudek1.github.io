How We Met

We lived next door to each other during our senior years at Michigan. Though we became good friends, we didn't begin dating until several years later. Emilee will tell you Eric was 'too weird' in college, while Eric suffered from a perpetual fear of commitment. After college Eric moved off to Chicago, while Emilee spent another year in nursing school and then later took a job in Petoskey, MI. We hung out intermittently over the next few years, but it wasn't until Emilee moved to Chicago in June 2014 that we went on our first date. We went to Shedd Aquarium, then grabbed dinner and drinks, and the rest is history!

The Proposal

On the morning of August 12, 2017 we were in Ann Arbor visiting with Emilee's sister Maddie. Before heading back to Chicago, Eric really wanted to spend some time walking around Ann Arbor with our dog Desmond. Specifically, Eric suggested that we walk through the Arb, a huge park right on campus, which coincidentally he had never been to before. We walked through the park for nearly 30 minutes before we got to an open area right alongside the Huron River. While Emilee wasn't looking, Eric got down on one knee and when Emilee turned around he popped the question! Unbeknownst to Emilee, Maddie had been hiding just around the corner and took pictures of everything. Meanwhile, Desmond was so excited that he temporarily forgot he hates water and decided to jump in the Huron River to celebrate!
